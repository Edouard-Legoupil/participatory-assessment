Refugees Participatory Assessment - Topic Modelling

A topic model is a type of statistical model for discovering the abstract "topics" that occur in a collection of documents. A topic model captures the intuition of word occurrence and combination in a mathematical framework, which allows examining a set of documents and discovering, based on the statistics of the words in each, what the topics might be and what each document's balance of topics is.
